Large Group

We meet as a fellowship every Friday to partake in dinner together, worship the Lord, and engage in thoughtful Bible Study. Our Mission Statement as Asian Christian Fellowship seeks to carry out the Great Commission of our Lord Jesus Christ to make disciples of all nations by proclaiming the gospel, providing fellowship for edification, nurturing Christians in spiritual growth, and building up Christian leaders.

What We Do

Dinner

At 6PM every Friday, we catch up with each and enjoy wonderful food that our God has graciously prepared for us.

Worship

Led by our Exalt Worship Team, we sing and glorify the Lord through praise and music.

Bible Study

After a short message, we gather together in smaller groups to discuss the word and see how we can live a better and more glorifying Christian lifestyle.
